MuhArch - Arch Linux made beautiful.

MuhArch provides a fully working minimal Arch Linux installation with an easy installer. There are a bunch of wallpapers in the Wallpaper folder and colorschemes are based on them. Simply do “wal -i /path/to/img” to change it to whatever you please. This is a minimal medium tailored to MY needs and usage. Further customization and changes are encouraged and expected from the user.
